O’Malley’s Tucson's Best Sports Bars O’Malley’s O’Malley’s (520) 623-8600 247 N 4th Ave, Tucson, AZ 85705

Looking for sports bars to cheer on all the local teams? Then head to O’Malley’s! O’Malley’s is a sports lover’s dream. All the usual foods for watching your favorite Arizona team, plus many you may not have thought of! I can’t go there without having the savory chili cheese fries and the boneless wings. Do you like spicy? Then try the Spicy Blackened Cajun Burger. They have a selection of “Only At O’Malley’s” drinks. Try a Get Lucky Trash Can or a Tennessee Honey Smash. Not that adventurous? Try a Pina Colada, Margarita, Cosmopolitan or a Harvey Wallbanger to name just a few.
