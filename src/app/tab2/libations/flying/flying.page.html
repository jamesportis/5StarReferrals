Flying Leap Plaza Palomino Flying Leap Flying Leap (520) 455-5499 2940 N Swan Rd Ste 128, Tucson, AZ 85712

This venues wine tasting rooms are spacious and well-lit. You can sit at the Mesquite tasting bar or cozy into one of the cushy sofas. It is also an art gallery. They feature pieces from local artists. Flying Leap Vineyards grow a vast portfolio of French, Spanish and Italian winegrapes. They also offer a family of premium dry rubs and seasonings, from the highest quality, premium spices available.
