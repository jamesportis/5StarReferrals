Playground Tucson's Best Sports Bars Playground Bar and Restaurant Playground Bar and Restaurant (520) 396-3691 278 E Congress St, Tucson, AZ 85701

The Playground is located in the heart of Downtown Tucson, making it the perfect “go to” spot for watching your favorite sporting event. You can sit indoors and watch one of their many TV screens, or outside on their large screen TV, located right behind the outdoor bar, so you won’t have to miss a play! Some of the house favorites include chili, tostitos, sandwiches, burgers, wings and much, much more!
