Arizona Wine Collective is an unique experience. Yes, they take you on a journey. They offering tastings on five different wines of your choice. They also offer Arizona-produced beers on tap. The beers are really delicious, so please be sure to try them. Are you the designated driver? No worries, they offer many non-alcoholic options including Kombucha from Cottonwood. Want a snack? They offer locally produced snacks. Snacks usually include assorted cheeses and popcorn. If you want to feast on a meal, you are invited to bring your own meal with you from home or one of the local, neighborhood restaurants.
