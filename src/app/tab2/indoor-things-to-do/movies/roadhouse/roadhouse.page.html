Roadhouse Cinemas Tucson's Best Movie Locations Roadhouse Cinemas Roadhouse Cinemas (520) 468-7980 4811 E Grant Rd #150, Tucson, AZ 85712

This theatre offers deep, reclining seats and at-your-seat food and drink service. They even offer alcoholic options for the adults in the group! They offer Specials on prices such as Early Bird Special, $6 Tuesdays, Wise Wednesdays for those 55+ and Industry Night Mondays, featuring discounts on drinks and snacks.
