Old Town Artisans Tucson's Best Shopping Old Town Artisans Market Old Town Artisans Market (520) 623-6024 201 N Court Ave, Tucson, AZ 85701

If you’re looking for a true, historic Tucson shopping experience, then you must visit the distinctive shops and galleries located inside this 150-year-old adobe building. It’s located in the El Presidion Historic District. You’ll find crafts and jewelry from hundreds of local and regional artists. You can also enjoy lunch and live music at the La Cocina. La Cocina is located in the beautiful courtyard.
