Charrro Del Rey is part of the Charro Restaurant Family, located next door from Charro Steak. The portions are very generous and delectable. The paired sauces compliment the dishes perfectly. I definitely recommend that you end the evening with the Champagne Fried Strawberries. They are topped with a whiskey cream glaze! It’s light and tasty!

Tony Ray :)

More About This Referral:

Charro del Rey is Tucson’s “galería de mariscos”, a gallery-like menú of classic and delicious “Charro Family” takes on seafood and prime dishes that range from the staples of legendary coastal dinner house menus of our childhood to the marinated mariscos made famous in such Mexican regions as Sinaloa and Sonora.
