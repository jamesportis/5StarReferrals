Charro Steak Steakhouse Charro Steak Charro Steak (520) 485-1922 188 E Broadway Tucson, AZ 85701

Run by the same family as the El Charro Mexican Restaurant, you can expect the same high-quality fine dining service and food. You must start your meal with the Guacamole Tableside. You can order it mild or picante. (I like it hot, so I suggest picante). The steaks are all-natural cuts with no antibiotics or added hormones, so the taste explodes in your mouth. If you still have room for dessert, then I would suggest the Dulceria Sample Board, so that your entire table can enjoy each of the wonderful desserts.
