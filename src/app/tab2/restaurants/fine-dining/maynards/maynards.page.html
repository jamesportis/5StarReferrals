Maynards offers what Tucson fine dining is all about. Great service, spectacular ambiance and exceptional food selections. If you arrive early (before your friends) make sure to take a seat at the bar and enjoy one of their scrumptious drinks. I had an Amoroso Sour and it was by the best one I ever had. After you finish (if you can) your amazing dinner, you must have the chocolate torte for dessert. Be prepared to share, because you won’t be able to finish it by yourself!

Tony Ray :)
