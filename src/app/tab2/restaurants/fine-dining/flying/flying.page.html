Flying V Bar & Grill Ventana Canyon Flying V Bar & Grill Ventana Canyon - Flying V Bar & Grill (520) 299-2020 7000 N Resort Dr, Tucson, AZ 85750

Modern American cuisine splashed with a Southwestern flair. I don’t know which is more pleasing, the delicious fine dining food or the stunning views of the Catalina Mountains. If you love Tequilas, then you will be happy to hear that they have almost 70 tequilas and cocktails available for your pleasure. The tableside guacamole is a must! It’s made fresh with both traditional and non-traditional ingredients.

Tony Ray :)
