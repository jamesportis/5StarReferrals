Senae Thai Thai Restaurant Senae Thai Bistro Senae Thai (520) 373-5335 63 E Congress Street, Tucson AZ 85701

Not a curry fan, well, it was just okay. Then one day Dee, owner of Senae Thai Bistro, opened her new Thai restaurant in downtown Tucson and my taste buds were challenged. The curries (red, green and yellow) are all delicious.

They have a level of sophistication you may not have ever tasted before. The layers of flavors keep you wanting more. I am convinced there is some sort of addictive drug in the sauce. I can never get enough. Try everything. This is our absolute favorite Thai restaurant.

If you get to meet Dee, and you might because she likes to greet her guests, tell her that Darren and Tony Ray referred you.

More About This Referral:

Amonwadee “Dee” Buizer, Chef and Owner of Senae Thai Bistro is pleased to bring over 30 years of experience as a very successful restaurateur to downtown Tucson.  Senae Thai Bistro offers exquisite, traditional Thai cuisine that is beautifully presented, and exceptionally served.

Our carefully chosen wine, beer and cocktail selections are perfect complements to her dishes.
