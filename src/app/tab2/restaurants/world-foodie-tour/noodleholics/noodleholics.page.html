Noodleholics Chinese Restaurant Noodleholics Noodleholics (520) 305-4262 3502 E Grant Rd, Tucson, AZ 85716

Noodleholics is one of my favorite noodle places here in Tucson. This Little gem is a family own restaurant where two amazing cultures come together, Korean and Chinese.

Noodlehlics brings us the best of the Guilin region with their rice noodles and their pickled green beans, bamboo, peanuts, and other spices. This dish can be ordered with broth and no broth, you will not be disappointed with either of those, but during winter there is nothing better than their “soups”.

You must try their house made wheat noodles. If you go during the weekend make sure you are early, since these noodles are made fresh every day, they tend to run out very fast.

But their menu does not end there, they also have small dishes like dumplings, fried wings, veggie rolls and Rice plates. Once you try their food you will understand their name!

For beginners I recommend My favorite Noodleholics dish: the Dry Guilin Noodles with crispy pork and Pig intestine. Okay okay, if you are not into that, you have many other choices of meat to choose from. Every noodle dish comes with 2 choices of meats. Oh, and do not forget your side dish of Bok Choy, you will thank me later!

Marta
