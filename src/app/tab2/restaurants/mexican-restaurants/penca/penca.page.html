Penca Mexican City meets Pre-Columbia Penca Penca (520) 203-7681 50 E Broadway Blvd, Tucson, AZ 85701

Owners Patricia and Ron opened Penca in 2013. They offer traditional Mexican cuisine, mixed with the influence of Spanish and French flavors. Start your meal off with delicious, home made tortilla chips and flavorful salsa. The service is outstanding, enhancing the entire dining experience. The food is creative and beautifully presented. Make sure to try one of their delicious Sangria’s. Buy it by the jar, because a glass won’t be enough!

More About This Referral:

In 2013 Patricia Schwabe with her husband Ron Schwabe decided to bring the flavors of traditional Mexican cuisine to the old building in downtown Tucson that they were getting ready to renovate. Patricia grew up with the influence of her mother’s kitchen, a traditional Mexico City cuisine, full of flavors inspired by the pre-colombian ingredients, mixed with the influence of the Spanish and French.
