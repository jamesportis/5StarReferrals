Elvira’s is nestled in the heart of the Downtown area. It has a second location in Tubac if you were planning on hitting the road for an adventure. The restaurant features many delicious and unique items. One of the fan favorites is the mole.

More About This Referral:

Established in 1927 Elvira's Restaurant of Nogales, Mexico is now in Tubac, Arizona bringing you the best Mexican cuisine and award-winning dishes! The third location has opened in Downtown Tucson and the decor is as breathtaking as the food is delicious.

Whatever your craving, they will have a dish to please you. The stunning lights at the Downtown location make you feel as though you are eating under the stars.
