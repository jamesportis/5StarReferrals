Mural Hunting Tucson's Best Murals Go Mural Hunting in Tucson City of Tucson Mural Program

One of the best ways you can get to know the culture of Tucson is to visit the numerous murals found in Downtown Tucson and the surrounding areas.  In fact, mural hunting happens to be one of my favorite past-times and is a great opportunity to get outside while social distancing. What are you waiting for?  Grab your mask, hop on the road, and head downtown to see some of the most wonderful murals in the world!

Tony Ray
